E-Waste Management System (Tentative Machine)
            Less manual work will be required for salvaging valuable materials and electronics parts for reuse.            
     Helps in reducing large amount of E-waste generated every year and disposed by incorrect method.
            This system will help us to send maximum amount of e-waste to proper recycling centers.
There will be decrease in e-waste generation to recycling ratio.

COMPONENTS REQUIRED
•Raspberry pi
•Camera Module
•Servo Motor
•Display
•Ultrasonic Sensor
•Weight Sensor

PROTOTYPE
Using Web App / Mobile app demonstrating classification.
